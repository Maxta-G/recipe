Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar : 150 g

Butter : 200 g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80 g

Instruction

1

Preheat oven to 180°C

2

Mix dry ingredients

3

Add wet ingredients and stir

4

Pour into baking pan

5

Bake until done and cool
